About Us

We are a solar system integrator in Sri Lanka. We provide industrial, commercial, residential, and utility scale solar solutions, consultancy and project management services, and vending of solar and electrical components. We guarantee you services of most experienced and best qualified engineers in the industry. FirstEnergy also provides services to other Solar Power system integrators in Sri Lanka through its component Sale business. FirstEnergy is a subsidiary of Pacific Hydro Electric SL (Pvt) Limited, a diversified group of business which operates in Renewable Sector and Potable and Sewerage Water Sector in Sri Lanka for the past 15 years.
